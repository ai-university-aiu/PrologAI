Chapter 364 — Affect and the Context-Aware Membership Contract (Wave 10 Stage 2)

This chapter adds two things that, together, close the Requirements Ledger's
AMYGDALA-1 finding: a first-class affective state, and a membership contract that
can read it.

Plain-language idea. A mind's later choices are coloured by how it feels now - a
held mood or stress tone. Before this chapter, PrologAI had no place to KEEP that
feeling, so a region that wanted a later step's legality to depend on stress had
to SMUGGLE the stress reading into the value it produced (an appraisal carrying
its own regime). That is a leak: the value now means two things at once.

The affective_state pack (WP-430). This is a single held context - a small record
(a dict) of a valence (threat, safe, appetitive, neutral), a salience (0.0 to
1.0), a mood, and a cortisol tone (0.0 to 1.0). It persists across calls, and it
derives a regime - baseline when calm, stress when the cortisol tone crosses a
threshold - that later processing reads. The public operations are:

 • affective_state_baseline(-State) - the neutral resting state.
 • affective_state_set(+State) - hold a full state.
 • affective_state_get(-State) - read the held state.
 • affective_state_regime(-Regime) - baseline or stress, from the held tone.
 • affective_state_modulate(+Delta) - fold a partial update in.
 • affective_state_decay - relax the tone one step toward baseline.
 • affective_state_clear - reset to baseline.

It is base infrastructure (layer 0), depends only on SWI-Prolog standard
libraries, and touches no ARC state.

The context-aware accessor (WP-431). The membership_contract pack already refuses
an output that is not a member of an offered set. This chapter adds a form whose
membership TEST is called with two arguments - the committed output AND a held
context read at check time:

    membership_contract_enforce_context(:Pred, +OutPos, :TestGoal,
                                        :ContextGoal, +Abstention, +Mode)

The ContextGoal (for example affective_state_regime) yields the held context; the
TestGoal decides whether the output is legal IN that context. So legality can
depend on how the mind feels without that feeling being carried inside the value.

Worked closure of AMYGDALA-1. A stand-in appraisal produces appraisal(Valence,
Salience) - and nothing else; the regime is not in the value. A context contract
says an appetitive appraisal is legal at baseline but not under stress. With a
stress state held, the appetitive appraisal is refused; cleared to baseline, it
passes. The amygdala's old regime-smuggling is now unnecessary.
