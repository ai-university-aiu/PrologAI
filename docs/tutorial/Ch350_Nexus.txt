Chapter 350: The Integration Layer - The nexus Pack (nx_*, Layer 365)

The seven AGI Foundations packs of Chapters 343 through 349 were each built
as a self-contained capability. A capability that sits on a shelf, however,
is not yet a mind. This chapter is about the wiring that makes those
capabilities participate in PrologAI's living cognition - the nexus pack.

Two ideas make nexus possible and keep it honest.

First, the layer rule. Every pack has a layer number, and a pack may only
call packs strictly below it. The workspace lives near the bottom of the
stack (its broadcast is one of the oldest parts of the mind); the J-Space
concept workspace lives near the top, at Layer 363. So the workspace cannot
call J-Space - that would be a low floor reaching up to a high one, which
the acyclic rule forbids. The solution is a capstone: nexus sits at Layer
365, above everything it touches, and reaches DOWN to both sides. Nothing
ever depends on nexus, so the building of packs still rests on tested
foundations all the way up.

Second, nexus adds no cognition of its own. It is pure plumbing. Every
junction is a named, inspectable predicate, and nexus_wirings/1 will tell you
exactly what it connects:

    ?- nexus_wirings(W).
    W = [ wiring(jspace_from_workspace, ...),
          wiring(curiosity_from_worldmodel, ...),
          wiring(agency_with_planner, ...),
          wiring(evolve_with_refinery, ...) ].

THE FOUR WIRINGS

Wiring one makes the J-Lens readout live. The workspace already lets any
actor subscribe to its broadcast bus; nexus subscribes a hook that, on every
broadcast, holds the winning coalition's concepts in a J-Space workspace at
the broadcast's salience.

    ?- jacobian_space_open(mind), nexus_bind_workspace(mind).

Now, whenever the workspace broadcasts, mind holds what the mind is
broadcasting, ranked by salience - the readable concept workspace of Chapter
348, updated in real time by the attention arbiter of the global workspace.
nexus_replay_broadcasts/2 lets you feed a whole reasoning episode offline, and
nexus_broadcast_reading/2 shows the result.

Wiring two turns the world model into a curiosity signal. The curiosity pack
learns from prediction error and rewards learning PROGRESS, not raw error.
nexus feeds it the world model's novelty: as an agent explores and a region
becomes familiar, novelty falls, and falling novelty is exactly what
curiosity reads as progress.

    ?- nexus_curiosity_explore(kitchen,
           [[at(door)], [at(door),saw(stove)], [at(door),saw(stove)]], P).
    P = 0.5.     % positive — the agent is learning the kitchen

A room full of unrelated novelties keeps novelty flat and returns zero
progress: the built-in guard against being mesmerized by noise.

Wiring three gives the agency loop a planner. The agency loop runs
observe-reason-act with a step budget; its reason-goal is a predicate that
decides what to do next. nexus supplies a reason-goal that decomposes the
current goal with planner_plan, and one that additionally checks the plan against
the current world with planner_monitor and replans when the world has moved.

    ?- nexus_plan_and_run(Domain, State, travel(home, park), 5, Outcome).
    Outcome = done(achieved(travel(home,park), [walk(home,park)])).

If no plan exists, the loop does not invent one - it escalates to human
oversight (no_plan, or cannot_recover after a failed replan). That
escalation path is the safety valve of the whole wiring.

Wiring four lets the mind evolve its own reasoning. The refinery scores an
output by how many criteria it passes; the evolve pack searches by fitness.
nexus makes the refinery score the fitness, so evolution improves reasoning
models under critique, within a bounded generation budget.

    ?- nexus_evolve_with_critique(Criteria, Params, 24, 5, 80, 1.0, 5, Best, S, G).
    Best = [observe, deduce, observe, induce, conclude], S = 1.0, G = 6.

Six generations of variation and selection assembled a valid
observe-deduce-conclude reasoning trace that no founder possessed - self
improvement, glass-box and reproducible.

WHY IT MATTERS

Each wiring is a single predicate you can read, test, and trust. Together
they turn seven shelved capabilities into a mind that broadcasts what it is
thinking into a readable workspace, grows curious about what it is learning,
plans and recovers toward its goals, and improves its own reasoning - all
without a single pack ever depending on something above it.

All 22 acceptance tests pass, including an end-to-end test in which the
workspace's own broadcast-subscriber list dispatches concepts into a
J-Space. The companion Mentova accomplishment, Acc_424, puts all seven
foundations to work in one connected episode of a curious agent's cognition.
