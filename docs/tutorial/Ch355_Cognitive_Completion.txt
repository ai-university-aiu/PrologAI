================================================================================
Chapter 355: The Cognitive Completion Suite - Eight Packs That Make the
Causalontology Family a Mind (co_*, Layers 384 through 391)
================================================================================

Up to this point the co_ (Causalontology) family was deep on one path: perceive
a scene, learn causes by doing, plan safely, explore, verify, and model the
world - everything an agent needs to attack the interactive ARC-AGI-3 benchmark.
But a benchmark solver is not yet a mind. A body of work on how to build
artificial minds (referred to internally as THE_BUILDING_FILES) lays out the
faculties a complete cognitive architecture needs: memory of lived experience,
attention, motivation, feeling, self-monitoring, safety, repair, and forgetting.
A gap analysis mapped those faculties onto the existing co_ packs and found eight
of them missing from the family. This chapter introduces the eight packs that
fill those gaps.

A note on naming. To differentiate this work from THE_BUILDING_FILES, none of the
new packs reuse the source's concept names. We keep the idea and the
architecture and give each a new, original name. Every pack is self-contained
(it needs nothing beyond the standard library), glass-box (every answer is a
named rule), carries an English comment above each line of code, and ships a
passing test suite.


co_trace - Episodic Memory (WP-409, Layer 384)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The mind kept no record of past episodes it could be reminded of. co_trace is
that record. Each experience is a small reified case:

    episode(Id, Context, Action, Outcome, Valence, Recency, Hits)

Context is a set of feature atoms describing the situation; Valence is how
good or bad it was, in the band minus-one to plus-one. Recall is by feature
overlap: the similarity of a present cue to a stored case is the Jaccard overlap
of their feature sets (shared features over total distinct features), so the most
relevant memory surfaces first. ct_recall returns the single best match and bumps
its hit count; ct_reinforce nudges a case's valence when it proves useful. This
is the glass-box analogue of an associative, case-based memory.


co_salience - Attention and Broadcast (WP-410, Layer 385)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A mind needs one integrating rhythm. Many specialists offer things that might
matter; a spotlight scores them; a bounded few are held in mind at once; and
exactly one winner is broadcast to the whole system each cognitive cycle. A
specialist offers an item with three plain numbers - novelty (surprise),
relevance (bearing on the goal), and affect (emotional charge) - and salience is
a transparent weighted sum in which affect counts by its magnitude, because both
a strong good and a strong bad feeling grab attention. sl_working_set is the best
K items the mind holds now; sl_broadcast is the single winner the rest of the
system reads.


co_impetus - Motivation (WP-411, Layer 386)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The family could infer an environment's goal (co_goalinfer) but had no goals of
its own, so it had no reason to act when none was handed to it. co_impetus gives
it that inner reason. A need is a quantity the system wants to hold at a target
set-point; the gap between target and the current reading is a pressure; and a
pressure becomes a goal to restore it. The agenda is ranked most-pressing-first
and is never empty: when every need is satisfied the mind still has a goal, to
explore, so an idle mind investigates rather than stalls.


affect - Affect (WP-412, Layer 387)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Feeling is not decoration: it steers attention and pre-selects choices before
slow deliberation. affect appraises each event on goal-congruence and
expectedness into a valence (how good or bad it felt) and an arousal (how
activating it was, rising with surprise and with the strength of the feeling). It
remembers each event's feeling, so an option under consideration can be
pre-flavoured by the feeling of the last similar outcome - the glass-box analogue
of a somatic marker. A running temperament is the mind's mood, and a coping
signal reads it as thriving, steady, or struggling.


co_gauge - Metacognition (WP-413, Layer 388)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A mind must watch itself work. co_gauge records the outcome of each attempt under
a named strategy, computes each strategy's calibration (success rate), and
notices the tell-tale of being stuck: enough attempts with a stubbornly low rate
is flagged as confusion, and a first-half-versus-second-half comparison of the
whole log reports whether the mind is improving, flat, or declining. Its single
recommendation is to use the best-calibrated strategy if one is good enough,
otherwise to seek guidance if it has tried and is not progressing, otherwise to
explore.


safety_governor - Safety Governor (WP-414, Layer 389)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A conscience must sit apart from the mind's own learning. safety_governor holds an
immutable constitution of forbidden action patterns (a pattern may carry a
variable, so forbidding touch of anything bans the whole family) plus learned
hazards mirrored in from co_core's preventive relations. It checks a proposed
action before it is taken and returns allow, or veto with a stated reason, and
every veto is appended to a log the mind's own learning cannot rewrite. There is
no predicate to weaken a constraint - the constitution is add-only within a
session and can be cleared only by an explicit operator reset.


repair - Repair (WP-415, Layer 390)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Planning avoids a hazard; repair recovers once a wrong thing has already
happened. In the Piagetian lineage of THE_BUILDING_FILES this equilibration move
is the central developmental mechanism. On a disturbance repair prefers, in
order, to undo the effect with an inverse action (inversion), else to cancel a
side effect with a neutralizer (neutralization), else to re-route a blocked goal
to an alternate (re-routing), else to accept it and move on. It classifies the
disturbance and grades the repair it achieves.


co_distill - Consolidation (WP-416, Layer 391)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
You cannot build a learning system without committing to forgetting. co_distill
is the night shift that keeps memory compact. Re-adding a record under the same
key merges rather than duplicates, bumping a use count and refreshing the value;
a reliably repeated sequence is compressed into one composite; and stale,
low-value records are forgotten - but a record is dropped only when it is both
under-used and old, so a fresh record is spared by a grace period and a well-used
old record is spared by its value.


Why the eight together matter
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
With these packs the Causalontology family spans every major faculty family in
THE_BUILDING_FILES: perception, memory (including the experiential and the
housekeeping kind), attention and a global broadcast, causal reasoning and
hypothesis, prediction and a world model, motivation, affect, planning and
action, learning, metacognition, compensation and equilibration, and safety and
governance. The two remaining honest gaps - a Causalontology-native social
cognition and a Causalontology-native language grounding - are named as the next
wave rather than hidden, in keeping with the honesty discipline of the whole
project. Every co_ pack lives in the PrologAI repository at
github.com/ai-university-aiu/PrologAI.
