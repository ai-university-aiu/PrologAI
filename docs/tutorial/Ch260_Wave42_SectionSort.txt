Chapter 260: Wave 42 - section_sort (WP-300, Layer 275)

section_sort solves ARC-AGI-2 task 78332cb0. All-6 divider rows and/or columns
partition the input grid into rectangular sections. The sections are sorted in
ascending order of their non-background, non-6 cell count. The output direction
is the transpose of the input direction (a vertical stack becomes a horizontal
row; a horizontal row becomes a vertical stack). For 2-dimensional inputs (both
row-dividers and column-dividers present), output direction is determined by
comparing the sum of section bounding-box heights against the sum of widths.
Single-cell 6-dividers are re-inserted between sections in the output.
