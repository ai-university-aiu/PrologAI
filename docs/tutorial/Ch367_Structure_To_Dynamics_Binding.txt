Chapter 367 — Binding Structure to Dynamics: realization (Wave 10 Stage 5)

This chapter closes the Requirements Ledger's Theme C - the structure-to-dynamics seam,
also called the grounding fit. With it, the four load-bearing walls the program named
(temporal, cross-stratal seam, structure-to-dynamics, affect) are all closed.

Plain-language idea. Everything in the mind is two things at once. A hormone is a
STRUCTURE (a record that says "this thing crosses these levels and suppresses that")
AND a piece of running CODE (a native law that computes the actual suppression). The
right way to handle this - proven earlier - is to ground the structure but keep the
dynamics as fast native code. But then the two halves were tied together only by
sharing an English name; nothing said, by identity, "THIS record is realized by THAT
law." So you could not trace from what a synapse computes to how it computes it, and a
mislabelled or missing law would go unnoticed.

The realization pack (WP-434, layer 0) is that missing tie. You bind a structure
record's id to the thing that realizes it:

 • native_law(Name/Arity) or native_law(Module:Name/Arity) - a named native predicate
   (the suppression law, the three-factor plasticity law).
 • lattice_signal(Nexus, Relation) - a typed signal on the shared Lattice carrying a
   value, a source port, and a timestamp (a neurotransmitter signal like dopamine).

The important part is that the tie is REAL, not just a label. Before you trust it, the
pack can check that the named law is actually defined (or the signal's nexus is open).
If a binding points at a law that does not exist, realization_check reports it as a
finding - dangling - instead of pretending the trace holds. realization_trace prints
the whole tie: this record, its realizer, and whether that realizer exists.

Why this also heals a structural problem. The native dynamics are, correctly, NOT
grounded, so they have no level (no stratum). That means structure and dynamics can
never live in the same one-level pack - the seam is unavoidable. The binding is exactly
the cross-cut that heals it without a shared level: the realization pack declares no
stratum, and the tie works across the seam all the same.
