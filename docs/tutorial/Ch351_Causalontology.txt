Chapter 351: Causalontology - The noun_backbone, realizable_hinge, causal_core, causal_learning, co_plan, and arc3_harness Packs (co_*, Layers 366-371)

A general intelligence must do more than recognize what things are. It must
model how and why things happen - predict the consequences of its actions,
diagnose the causes of what it observes, plan its way to goals, and avoid
harm. Causalontology is PrologAI's answer: an explicit, learnable,
hierarchical model of cause and effect, acquired by doing rather than merely
by observing, realized as six packs.

THE OLDEST BOUNDARY IN ONTOLOGY

The world divides into things that endure - nouns, continuants - and things
that unfold and bring other things about - verbs, occurrents. Causalontology
governs the verbs, defers on the nouns, and joins the two with a hinge.

The noun_backbone pack is the thin noun backbone: continuants with categories,
acyclic is-a and part-of hierarchies, and confidence-scored alignment so an
external ontology's classes can pivot onto the backbone without anyone
abandoning their own work.

The realizable_hinge pack holds the realizables - dispositions, functions, and
roles - which are noun-shaped (they inhere in objects) yet verb-facing
(they are realized in processes). The realization seam is queryable both
ways: from a button's pressable disposition to the pressing that realizes
it, and back.

THE FUNDAMENTAL UNIT

The causal_core pack reifies causation itself. Its unit is the Causal Relation
Object:

    cro(Id, Causes, Effects,
        temporal(Dmin, Dmax, Unit),   % the delay window IS the mechanism
        Modality,                     % necessary|sufficient|contributory|preventive
        Strength,                     % in [0,1]
        Context,
        prov(Source, Evidence, Conf)).

Three disciplines are enforced. First, mere sequence is never production:

    ?- causal_core_precedes_add(rooster_crow, sunrise),
       causal_core_after_but_not_because(rooster_crow, sunrise).
    true.    % the rooster does not cause the dawn

Second, timing is mechanism. The clinician's question - "how long ago did
you eat?" - becomes a gate: a candidate cause is admitted only if the
elapsed time falls inside its window.

    ?- causal_core_temporal_abduction(state(gastroenteritis),
           [ate(spoiled_shellfish)-elapsed(3, hours),
            ate(undercooked_poultry)-elapsed(2, hours)], Ranked).
    Ranked = [0.7-ate(spoiled_shellfish)].
    % the poultry is excluded PURELY on timing: two hours is before its window

Third, mechanisms decompose. A coarse relation carries a sub-graph of finer
relations, and causal_core_hierarchy_consistent checks that the parts genuinely chain
from the parent's causes to its effects.

And the subsumption argument is executable: causal_core_import_external turns a bare
external relation like ConceptNet's "Causes" into a degenerate provisional
CRO - some fields unspecified - which causal_core_refine_import then fills. One may
replace a representation only if one already contains it.

LEARNING BY DOING

The causal_learning pack acquires structure by intervention. The agent acts,
observes, and induces a relation at strength 0.70; a repeated intervention
confirms it to 0.90 and beyond, capped at 0.99. On first inducing
press(b_red), it posits on the noun side the pressable disposition the
verb-side causation revealed - the hinge populated from the bottom up. Harm
is tagged preventive and enters a closed-world avoid-set; null effects are
counted compactly rather than reified; and a relation that was merely
observed, never intervened on, is flagged observational and weighted down
to 0.30. Doing beats seeing, and the provenance says which was which.

PLANNING, SAFELY

The co_plan pack composes procedures - relations whose cause is a sequence -
and plans backward from a goal, refusing any plan through an avoided action.
Where no procedure exists, co_plan_chain assembles one by bounded backward
search over the causal graph:

    ?- co_plan_chain(light(on), 5, Plan).
    Plan = [flip(switch), power(on)].

THE HARNESS

The arc3_harness pack closes the loop against interactive environments - the
shape of the ARC-AGI-3 benchmark, where an agent is dropped without
instructions and must discover the mechanics by acting. An environment is a
pluggable quadruple of goals (reset, act, actions, solved), frames become
occurrents, frame diffs become induced relations, and action selection is
plan-first with curiosity fallback - never a learned hazard.

In the acceptance suite, the harness faces a mock volume-control game whose
mechanics it has never seen. It tries the innocuous action, touches the
spike exactly once, tags it preventive, never touches it again, discovers
that the transfer control raises the level, and wins within budget - with a
glass-box trace of every step. A guarded HTTP bridge speaks the same
environment shape to the live ARC-AGI-3 REST API; the benchmark itself
remains, as the source document insists, a labeled and falsifiable bet.

All 44 acceptance tests pass across the six packs, including the
specification's cross-layer query: an object that bears a realizable that
is realized in a process that participates in a causal relation, traversed
from noun to hinge to verb to predicted effect in one glass-box query.
