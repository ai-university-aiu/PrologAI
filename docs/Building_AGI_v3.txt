2. THE CENTRAL DESIGN PROBLEM: THE LAYER RULE

Every pack in PrologAI carries a layer number.

A pack at layer N may call predicates only from packs at a layer strictly below N.

This keeps the dependency graph acyclic (free of cycles), so packs build from the bottom up like floors in a building.

The four in-platform wirings connect NEW high-layer packs to OLD low-layer packs:

• jacobian_space is at Layer 363; the workspace is a substrate pack far below it.

• worldmodel is at Layer 360; curiosity is a substrate pack far below it.

• planner is at Layer 361; agency is a substrate pack far below it.

• evolve is at Layer 362; refinery is a substrate pack far below it.

A naive wiring would make the low pack reach up to the high pack, which the layer rule forbids.

The correct solution is a capstone - a single new pack that sits ABOVE everything it touches and reaches DOWN to both sides.

That capstone is the nexus pack, at Layer 365, above all seven foundations (Layers 358 through 364).

Because nothing depends on nexus, the acyclic order is preserved, and the wirings are legal.


3. WHAT WAS BUILT, PART ONE: THE nexus PACK (PrologAI)

The nexus pack (nx_ prefix, Layer 365, Work Package 390) is the integration layer.

It adds no cognition of its own.

It is pure plumbing (connecting logic), and every junction is a named, inspectable predicate.

The predicate nx_wirings returns a glass-box description of the four wirings it provides.

WIRING ONE - jacobian_space from the workspace broadcast:

The workspace already lets any actor subscribe to its broadcast bus.

nexus subscribes a hook, nx_hold_broadcast, that on every broadcast holds the winning coalition's concepts in a J-Space (Jacobian Space) workspace at the broadcast salience (importance score), clamped to the range zero to one.

The result is that the readable J-Lens readout is live during reasoning: whatever the mind broadcasts, it is holding, ranked by salience.

Tested end to end using the workspace's own broadcast-subscriber list.

WIRING TWO - curiosity from world-model novelty:

The curiosity pack learns from prediction error and rewards learning PROGRESS, not raw error.

nexus feeds it the world model's novelty score: as a region becomes familiar, novelty falls, and falling novelty reads as positive learning progress.

A trajectory of unrelated novel states keeps novelty flat and returns zero progress, which is the built-in guard against being mesmerized by noise.

WIRING THREE - the agency loop with the planner:

The agency loop runs an observe-reason-act cycle with a step budget.

nexus supplies a reason-goal that decomposes the current goal with ht_plan and, in the fuller version, checks the plan against the current world with ht_monitor and replans on failure.

When no plan exists, or recovery is impossible, the loop escalates to human oversight rather than inventing an answer - the safety valve of the wiring.

WIRING FOUR - evolution under refinery critique:

The refinery scores an output by how many criteria it passes.

nexus makes that score the fitness function for the evolve pack, so evolution improves reasoning models under critique, within a bounded generation budget.

In the acceptance test, six generations of variation and selection assemble a valid observe-deduce-conclude reasoning trace (from seed 5) that no member of the founding population possessed.


4. WHAT WAS BUILT, PART TWO: ACCOMPLISHMENT 424 (Mentova)

The fifth next step is a Mentova accomplishment, numbered Acc_424.

It is a demonstration program, demos/agi_foundations_demo.pl, that exercises all seven AGI Foundations packs end to end in one coherent episode.

The protagonist is Mira, a curious agent who shares a small world with another agent, Nomi.

In one run, Mira:

• Plans a two-room fetch in her world model and verifies it by simulation (worldmodel).

• Grows curious as world-model novelty falls into learning progress (worldmodel novelty as a curiosity signal).

• Decomposes a travel goal into a plan and keeps the glass-box decomposition tree (planner).

• Values a cue at exactly one bit while a reward's location is unknown, then acts on it (active_inference).

• Imagines the but-for counterfactual that had she not taken the key, the chest would be locked (causal).

• Models Nomi's false belief in the Sally-Anne structure, knows the truth herself, and attributes the mistaken belief to Nomi (tom).

• Evolves a valid reasoning routine (evolve).

• Reports her own workspace honestly, revealing the one concept she held silently and never spoke - the awareness that she was being evaluated (jacobian_space).

The program prints PASS for all nine of its acceptance criteria and a final nine of nine tally.

It follows the Mentova convention of asserting its own acceptance criteria and printing PASS or FAIL, run with a single swipl command.

7. HOW THIS COMPLETES THE VISION

The first phase gave PrologAI seven new capabilities, but a capability on a shelf is not yet a mind.

This second phase is the composition: the four wirings turn shelved capabilities into a mind that broadcasts what it is thinking into a readable workspace, grows curious about what it is learning, plans and recovers toward its goals, and improves its own reasoning.

The Mentova accomplishment then proves the composition in one connected episode of an agent's cognition.

Everything remains glass-box: every junction and every step is a named, inspectable predicate, and the agent's own workspace is readable at the end, including the thought it kept to itself.
