COMMERCIAL LICENSING FOR PROLOGAI AND MENTOVA

This document describes the process for obtaining a Commercial License
for PrologAI and Mentova under The Attribution Always; No Profit, No Problem
License.


WHO NEEDS A COMMERCIAL LICENSE

You need a Commercial License if your use of PrologAI or Mentova:

• Generates revenue directly or indirectly for your organization.
• Serves paying customers or clients in any capacity.
• Is embedded in a product or service that charges users or generates
  income through advertising, subscriptions, or any other means.
• Provides a competitive or monetary advantage to a for-profit business.
• Results in financial benefit, including indirect benefits such as cost
  reduction in a revenue-generating context.

If you are a tinkerer, hobbyist, student, educator at an accredited
institution, non-profit organization, or independent academic researcher,
you do not need a Commercial License. See LICENSE.txt Rule 2 for full
free use terms.


WHAT A COMMERCIAL LICENSE INCLUDES

All Commercial License Agreements for PrologAI and Mentova include the
following standard elements:

1. Grant of rights

A written grant specifying exactly what you are licensed to do with
PrologAI and Mentova, including any restrictions on use, modification,
distribution, or sublicensing.

2. Royalty clause

A royalty clause specifying the percentage of gross profits your
organization will pay to D. R. Dison, derived from all use of PrologAI
or Mentova. The percentage is negotiated individually based on the scope
of use, the nature of the product or service, and the anticipated revenue.

3. Reporting and audit rights

A requirement to provide periodic profit and revenue reports, and the
right of D. R. Dison to audit those reports to verify accuracy.

4. Payment schedule

A defined payment schedule specifying when royalty payments are due,
the accepted methods of payment, and the currency of payment.

5. Attribution requirement

Confirmation that Rule 1 of the license (full attribution) remains in
force throughout the commercial license term.

6. Term and renewal

The duration of the license and the process for renewal or termination.

7. Governing law and dispute resolution

The jurisdiction and method for resolving any disputes arising from
the Commercial License Agreement.


HOW TO INITIATE A COMMERCIAL LICENSE INQUIRY

Step 1 - Contact D. R. Dison at ai.university.aiu@gmail.com

Use the subject line: "Commercial License Inquiry - PrologAI/Mentova"

Step 2 - Describe your intended use

In your inquiry, include:
• The name of your organization and your role.
• A description of the product or service you intend to build or offer.
• How PrologAI or Mentova will be used in that product or service.
• Your anticipated timeline for launch or commercial deployment.
• Any other context that will help assess the scope of your use.

Step 3 - Receive a response

D. R. Dison will respond to commercial inquiries within a reasonable
timeframe to initiate the negotiation process.

Step 4 - Negotiate and sign

The Commercial License Agreement will be drafted, negotiated, and signed
before any commercial use begins. Commencing commercial use before a
signed agreement is in place constitutes a violation of the license and
may result in legal action.


IMPORTANT NOTES

• There is no fixed commercial license fee or standard royalty rate.
  All terms are negotiated individually.

• Attribution to PrologAI, Mentova, AIU (Artificial Intelligence University), and D. R. Dison is always required,
  regardless of the terms of your individual Commercial License Agreement.

• Sublicensing of PrologAI or Mentova to third parties is not permitted
  without explicit written authorization in your Commercial License
  Agreement.

• A Commercial License for one product or service does not extend to
  other products or services unless explicitly stated.
